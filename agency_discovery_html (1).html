Agency Prioritization Scoring Rubric

Score agencies 0-60 points to determine follow-up priority

Technical Readiness Score (30 points max)

API Capability (0-10 points)
üü¢ 10 pts: Dev resources ready, existing API usage, 30-day timeline
üü° 5 pts: Has capability but needs education/support
üî¥ 0 pts: No dev resources, no API experience, 60+ days
Performance Optimizer Readiness (0-10 points)
üü¢ 10 pts: High volume, bandwidth, comfortable with automation
üü° 5 pts: Moderate volume, some automation, needs training
üî¥ 0 pts: Low volume, manual-only, no automation interest
IZA Fit (0-10 points)
üü¢ 10 pts: Clear apply quality pain, screening needs, enterprise clients
üü° 5 pts: Some quality concerns, moderate client fit
üî¥ 0 pts: No screening needs, volume-focused, SMB clients

Opportunity Size Score (30 points max)

Based on: Growth Potential + Competitive Spend

30 pts: $50K+ monthly opportunity

25 pts: $20K-$49K monthly opportunity

20 pts: $10K-$19K monthly opportunity

15 pts: $5K-$9K monthly opportunity

10 pts: Under $5K monthly opportunity

Calculation Example:
Current: $5K/mo | Growth: $8K/mo | Competitive: $12K/mo
Total = $8K + $12K = $20K/mo = 25 points

Priority Tiers & Actions

TIER 1: 50-60 points
High readiness + High opportunity = Immediate action
Action: Call within 7 days, assign AM support, fast-track onboarding
TIER 2: 35-49 points
Strong potential needs enablement OR ready but moderate opportunity
Action: 30-day nurture, education resources, quarterly check-ins
TIER 3: 20-34 points
Standard relationship maintenance
Action: Standard cadence, monitor triggers, annual review
TIER 4: Under 20 points
Long-term nurture, low immediate potential
Action: Minimal touch, automated updates, revisit if changes
